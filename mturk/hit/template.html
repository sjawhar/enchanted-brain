Overview

We are conducting a cognitive science experiment on music. We would like to understand which emotions a particular piece of music evokes in you. You will use our app to listen to a 2-3 minute excerpt of classical music and periodically tell us how it makes you feel. You will then be provided with a unique code to enter into the box at the bottom of this page, which will complete the assignment.

Instructions

To complete this task, you will need to use your mobile phone:

  1. Install the Expo app on your mobile phone.
  2. Once Expo is installed, open it and click "Scan QR Code" at the top of the screen.
  3. Scan the QR code below to load our app.
  4. When the app loads, you will see our terms and conditions. Review and accept these to continue.
  5. You will now see a brief demographics form. Please fill this out.
  6. After submitting the demographics form, the music will begin.

While listening to the music, you will be prompted every 20 seconds to evaluate how ${emotion} you feel the previous 20 seconds of music was. You will have five seconds to respond to each prompt.

When the excerpt ends, you will be given a unique code. Make sure to leave this window open as you complete the task. When you are finished, return to this page to paste the code into the box below.
